Automation of team schedules:
why and how does it work?

Minea

17 August 2021 - Updated 30.08.2021

Automation is the fact of using technologies or applications, software that will perform a low-value or repetitive task for you. Thanks to automation, these missions or “tasks” can be started or processed in just a few clicks, with less effort; And often, for maximum results! Since it is well known that the least rewarding tasks are also often those which take the most time, even if they are not pleasant, it is therefore essential to do them well.

👾 Digitization: a global trend

As said previously, more and more companies (and this does not only concern large groups!) Are deciding to digitize their organization to boost their business. This allows them, on the one hand, to clean up working time by putting aside boring tasks, but also to instill innovation by having more time for strategy. And we know how important originality and creativity are to stand out.

In a society where everything is always going faster and where performance is the key word, digitalization makes it possible to streamline processes and increase team productivity.

Companies are also going digital because they have no other choice: if all the competition does, they are forced to align in order to hope to offer a service or products superior or at least equal.

If we zoom out to look at HR management as a whole and precisely the planning of work schedules, we realize that automating it allows managers or managers, at their level, to focus on more tasks. high added value. And this, both for them and their personal progression towards success, and for the business interests of the company.

In personnel management, several aspects of the planning and communication of schedules can be automated.
